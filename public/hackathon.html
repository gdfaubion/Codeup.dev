Part 1!

Your traveling to a distance city that you've never been to before to find work. As you get close to the city, you hear what sounds like a small fight a little off the path. You venture closer to see what is happening. Three guards are trying to trap one man. The man looks to be a weary traveller like yourself, wearing cheap leather clothes, and also like yourself, has a descent sword. What do you do?. | 1) Continue to the city, this has nothing to do with you. | 2) Help the guards. Maybe they can help you get a job. | 3) Attack the guards, this isn't a fair fight, and you like to fight!
